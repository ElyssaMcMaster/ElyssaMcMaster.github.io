Beyond FA

I serve as lead organizer on Beyond FA, an international grand challenge with 17 other organizers and 50+ registered participants. We aim to identify Alzheimer's Disease imaging biomarkers and compare them in a common framework. I communicate directly with participants, curate the dataset, and demonstrate public versions of our baseline methods and testing code. The challenge will remain open through July 2025!
