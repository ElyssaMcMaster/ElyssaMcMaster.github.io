About Me

Hello! I'm Elyssa, a PhD student in electrical and computer engineering at Vanderbilt University. My research focuses on advanced image analysis methods and machine learning tools for complex biomedical data, with applications in brain structure and disease.

Beyond FA

I serve as lead organizer on Beyond FA, an international grand challenge with 17 other organizers and 50+ registered participants. We aim to identify Alzheimer's Disease imaging biomarkers and compare them in a common framework.
